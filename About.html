Challenging the Status Quo

We recognize that the world faces growing crises in the form of climate crisis and its destabilizing effects. Our solution? We understand the need to challenge the status quo, and question our assumptions about what is possible.

Traditional ways of designing are no longer good enough. Working with you, Glow Up does not choose between sustainability and beauty. We create purposeful designs that reflect your moral commitment to ensure our future.

Our Values

Beauty

Green is beautiful. Our planet is beautiful. Design is beautiful. And we know environmentally-friendly design practices create the most beautiful of pieces for our clients.

Education

We believe strongly that the most powerful tools we have in discussing climate crisis are knowledge-based. We take care to break down our practices online and with our clients.

Science

We’re not here to bullsh*t you. Everything we recommend has been thoroughly researched and evaluated using climate justice frameworks, peer-reviewed studies, and IPCC policy guidelines.

Honesty

We want our own sustainability efforts and impact to be as clear and transparent as possible. With sustainability it’s important to walk the talk.
